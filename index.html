Our Process

Meticulous attention to every detail

1

Initial Assessment

Thorough inspection and documentation of vehicle condition, paint readings, and specific areas requiring attention.

2

Surface Preparation

Professional washing, clay bar treatment, and surface decontamination to ensure a perfectly clean canvas.

3

Paint Correction

Multi-stage polishing process to remove imperfections and restore paint to its optimal condition.

4

Panel Inspection

Detailed examination under specialized lighting to ensure uniform finish across all surfaces.

5

Protection Application

Careful application of ceramic coating or paint protection film for long-lasting results.

6

Curing Process

Controlled environment curing to ensure maximum bonding and protection effectiveness.

7

Final Detailing

Meticulous attention to every detail including glass, trim, and wheels for complete perfection.

8

Quality Control

Final inspection under various lighting conditions and comprehensive documentation of completed work.
